# Project Brief: LandCare AI

## 1. Project Name

LandCare AI – Public Land Procedure Assistant

## 2. Author

Tôn Thất Minh Cường

## 3. Project Type

Personal, non-official AI prototype.

## 4. Problem

Citizens often receive administrative documents related to land acquisition, inventory, compensation, support, or resettlement but do not understand:

- what procedural stage they are in;
- what documents they need to prepare;
- what questions they should ask;
- how to write a basic request for clarification.

This creates an information gap between citizens and public administrative procedures.

## 5. Proposed Solution

LandCare AI uses AI to transform complex administrative text into:

- plain-language explanations;
- practical document checklists;
- suggested questions;
- draft request letters for reference.

## 6. MVP Scope

The MVP focuses on the first citizen-facing scenario:

> A citizen receives a land acquisition or inventory-related notice and wants to know what to do next.

## 7. Target Users

- Citizens affected by land acquisition or compensation procedures.
- Public-service support staff.
- Students or learners studying public land administration.

## 8. Data Policy

The prototype uses:

- public legal documents;
- synthetic sample cases;
- sample forms created for demonstration.

It does not use real citizen data or internal government documents.

## 9. Non-official Disclaimer

This project does not represent any government agency and does not provide official legal advice or administrative decisions.
