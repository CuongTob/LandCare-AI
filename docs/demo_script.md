# Demo Video Script - 3 Minutes

## 0:00 - 0:20 Problem

Many citizens receive land acquisition, compensation, or resettlement-related documents but do not understand what they mean or what to do next.

## 0:20 - 0:40 Solution

LandCare AI is a personal, non-official AI prototype that turns complex administrative language into plain-language explanations, checklists, and draft request letters.

## 0:40 - 1:20 Demo 1: Explain Notice

Paste a synthetic case:
"A household receives an inventory notice for a public project and does not know what documents to prepare."

Show the AI output:
- simple explanation;
- current stage;
- next steps.

## 1:20 - 2:00 Demo 2: Document Checklist

Switch to checklist mode.

Show:
- identity documents;
- land-related documents;
- property/tree/structure documents;
- questions to ask the competent authority.

## 2:00 - 2:40 Demo 3: Draft Letter

Switch to draft letter mode.

Show a short request letter asking for clarification and document guidance.

## 2:40 - 3:00 Safety and Impact

Emphasize:
- personal non-official prototype;
- public documents and synthetic cases only;
- no real citizen data;
- designed to reduce information gaps in public procedures.
